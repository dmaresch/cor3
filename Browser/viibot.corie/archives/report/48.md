## ASSESSMENT CT1702.1 - SHIPMENT CONTAINERS

RESTRICTED

**Date:** 2251.03.15

**Source:** CORIE Intelligence Network

**Assessment ID:** CT1702.1

**Intelligence Report:**

"Our guys took a closer look at those containers. The metal contains unknown residual radiation. It's strong enough to fry some electronic scanners. I wonder if that's what's been affecting everything in the facility recently …"

But that's not the main thing. Those containers have been in space for at least 30 years, and we think that's not their first rodeo. One of my techs suggested that those have been used in ship-to-ship deliveries of some sorts, and several got stranded in space somehow.

We found some markings on the inside. They look like they were made by some kind of automated system, not by hand. The patterns are too precise, too regular. And they're in a language we don't recognize.

My recommendation: we should get rid of those containers as soon as possible. They're a liability, and we don't know what kind of attention they might bring. The radiation alone is enough to cause problems, but the markings suggest there's more to this story than we're seeing."
