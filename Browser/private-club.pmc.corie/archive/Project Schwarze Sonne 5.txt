Date : -redacted-
Location: Camp  -redacted-. 

 

Fools. They do not understand what is at stake here.  “Experiments”.  Nein !  This is not an experiment, this is my child. I am the only one left …
My friends, Gunther, Sigurd, Ivan,  Marina - they all died.  No. This is not death, this is much worse. They still exist somewhere.  Erased. They are stuck in that verdammt limbo !  

I have to be cruel, I have to experiment.  That device should not have ever been used in the first place !  It erases one’s self. People become empty, dead inside. 

They want me to modify it, they want to control other people. They think that lower exposure levels can keep the conscience. It is not so. They all become puppets. Here but not here. 

But when did it all start … I struggle to remember. It must be exposure, I must have been hit as well.  

Red sand, red rocks. Bunker, deep. People in uniforms. Comrades … why can’t I recall their faces ? What happened to them ? 

I know one thing - I have to repair it. I have to make it work. I have to bring everyone back. It can do it, I know. Just a little bit more … experiments. Yes, I must continue.  I will prove my worth ! They will let me !  I know !  For everyone, for the greater good !
