**Document - Toxicology report (part of):**

The barrels found at the crime scene contain traces of unknown chemicals. A council security officer that got in contact with the substance without protective equipment received a dose which led to extensive hallucinations, vomiting, panic attacks and "magic horrors" as he described. The dose was enough to keep his mind in an altered state for almost 8 hours. The officer was transported to the local hospital and is still under strict monitoring. Permanent neurological damage is considered possible.

**News article: "A drug lab bust leads to mysterious findings"**

January 4 2247 - A counter-drug task force by Council Security has been dispatched to an "Flavius" asteroid base. Upon docking, the task force was encountered by a group of unknown armed people that opened fire as soon as the transport apparel opened.

In a short firefight the offenders were quickly dispatched with no Security council losses (allegedly). Inside the base, a drug lab has been found.

Due to the officers that were participating in a raid (all names and ranks are classified), the location belonged to the CDEA (colony drug enforcement agency) - one of the most famous drug cartels that was operating throughout the whole solar system.

Among the discovered drugs and chemicals, several barrels with "The Tantrum" label were found. The drug experts at the nearest patrol station were not able to identify the chemical components included in them, but the substance was deemed highly active and dangerous. The barrels were allegedly isolated in a secure vault for further investigation.
