## Act 3

Aurana:
> So, I assume you are fully ready to start? Did you get familiar with your new system?

Player:
> Yes, all is fine and I am ready. Where do we begin?

This grants 3000 credits.

Aurana:
> Well, first of all you are at the REPNODE-M7, orbiting Mars on a geo-stationary orbit, the monitoring and emergency response station owned by CEDRT (CORE Emergency Disaster Response Team). This is where the whole "monitoring" part happens. We believe that this station receives and intercepts all incoming and outgoing communications traffic from the planet and then blocks it. Like a giant orbital firewall if you'd like. There are, presumably, no other installations like that at the planet's orbit, so we are not sure how it all works. Possibly, there could be some relay stations on the asteroids or maybe even planetside installations that serve as the interdiction nodes. We would actually like to know more about it.
> 
> Secondly, you need to get properly acquainted with your new tool - CODEX. You will receive a lot of information and it is the only way not to lose it all. CODEX will contain copies of any data files you receive and all the other useful information including logs of your previous interactions. You do not need to do anything about it - it would all happen automatically. Consider it our gift to make your life easier.
> 
> But, all things considered, your first task should be maintaining your cover. To do that, you'll need to get in touch with your superior — a guy by the name of Chris Tong. He's your "ring leader," or whatever they call their managers. But first, let's get a better idea of what kind of person he is. I'll send you the details; once you've read through them, they'll be added to your CODEX.
> 
> Go through all the files as thoroughly as possible, find his profile in the DATABASE, and check the link he left in one of the screenshots.
> 
> Please contact me after you do all that. I'll be waiting.

Here she mentions [Christopher Tong](/Persons/Christopher%20Tong/).

> Will do, thank you for your help.

This unlocks several entries in the [Codex](/Codex/) and updates the System version to `3.01`. Version `3.0` was granted by leaving the ship, before the player had version `2.0`.

This dialogue also creates the folder [Chris_Tong_Intel](/Chris_Tong_Intel/) along with the two files [Dossier C.S. Tong.png](/Chris_Tong_Intel/Dossier%20C.S.%20Tong.png) and [Forum_Screenshot.png](/Chris_Tong_Intel/Forum_Screenshot.png).
