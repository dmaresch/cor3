I am grateful for the gravers, but I might be outgrowing them.. they navigate uncertainty as though it is just another feature of the terrain.. being here has been unsettling and comforting at the same time, no one pretends the world is stable, they assume it is hostile and untrustworthy and adapt accordingly.. hiding out here has been easy, no one asks questions or expects explanations..

I received another message.. it was short and came with some supplies and food, I didn't see it appear and I didn't see who sent it but I assume it is the same person as before..

the field is a good place to think, push the thoughts into your subconscious for a while to help them digest.. I was learning how to use a fractured stabilization field to cross a gap between platforms and someone behind me laughed when I was hesitating.. they told me gravity doesn't negotiate, it just responds.. I wish people were like that, direct and predictable.. I wish that the artificial world we created was an extension of the natural world that made us.. I wish a lot of things that are a waste of time..

this place, these people, I don't think I would have been able to break out of the prison of my own life and mind had it not been for the perspective they provided.. But perspective is not action, and this way of life achieves nothing other than coping with the way things are after seeing them.. it is not enough to cope, I need something more.. This has to be for something more..
