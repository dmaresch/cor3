Document Title: Security Incident Report - Executive Sector Breach

Reference ID: IR-447-E

Location: Executive Administration Wing

Date:

Reporting Officer: (Lt. Supervisor L. Varga)

Distribution: Internal Security Command, Executive Compliance Office

Incident Overview:

At approximately 22:14 station time, an unauthorized access alarm was triggered within the Executive admin wing. Originating from Office sector E-37-4 assigned to Senior Executive Douglas Flanders.

Security response units were dispatched immediately upon alarm activation.

Responding personnel identified evidence of attempted forced access to an interior data storage cabinet located in the office suite. No external structural damage to entry doors.

Unidentified individual was located inside the secured area. Individual was detained following brief resistance.

Detained individual was female, average height, dark clothing consistent with civilian attire, minor visible facial injuries sustained during containment. Subject did not provide identification when requested.

Security personnel report that subject escaped custody using an unknown route. Subsequent search operations were conducted across multiple levels. The subject was not located.

Property and Data Integrity:

Preliminary audit confirms that primary networks were not breached, no confirmed digital data transfer occurred, physical storage inventory complete

Risk assessment:

This incident represents a moderate internal security concern.
