there is a deafening silence that follows you when you step outside yourself and your life.. I have stopped expecting the familiar sounds, no more chimes announcing opening and closing doors, no more clawing of filtered air humming through sealed corridors.. Now there are only temporary beds, borrowed clothes.. I am going through everything that has happened again like a passage i looked over without reading, something so obvious and clear that i just never saw before because.. well maybe because I was too close to it..

someone sent me a message today.. no name or origin I could recognize.. it wasn't the same account that sent me the message about Jack earlier.. that his intentions were not pure.. but they mentioned Jack, that I had done the right thing, then sent me these intelligence reports.. they must have been written by him, there's no one else I was so close to.. Someone you think you know so well, and for what.? I wanted to throw them away but i read until the words lost shape.. I need to know why.. I need to know..

the trust i put in him was not a mistake, it was an outcome someone else had generated.. I have been so vain to not step out into the larger picture of things, but i have learned now, i have learned from my mistakes and understand the scale of things better..

we think of ourselves as different from the drones and bots that keep this machine turning, but that place showed me we are not, we are programmable machines that can be disposed of when defective..
