MO CHEMICALS – DIRECTORIAL CODE OF CONDUCT

All Directors must:

Maintain integrity of intake and waste documentation.

Ensure regulatory compliance.

Avoid procedural irregularities that could attract audit review.

Protect company interests above individual liability.

Maintain strict confidentiality.

Violation of compliance protocols may result in:

– Immediate termination
– Liability reassignment
– Legal escalation under USOL Industrial Regulation
