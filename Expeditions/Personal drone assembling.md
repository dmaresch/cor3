# Personal Drone Program

A long-term engineering project unlocking autonomous operations on Mars.

- **More raids**  
While your mercenaries are busy running orbital contracts, your drone can head to Mars on its own. It does not take up a hiring slot, does not need a salary, and runs in parallel with your main operations.  
In practice, that means one more extraction window working in the background: more raids, more resources, and faster account progression.
- **Mars-only expeditions**  
The drone gives you access to a location your mercenaries cannot reach – the Martian surface.  
That opens a separate layer of content built around surface expeditions: blueprints, rare components, records, and narrative artifacts that do not appear on orbital stations. Some things can only be found by sending the drone down.
- **Personal logistics**  
The built-in cargo bay turns your drone into a personal transport unit.  
Move goods between the station and the surface, bring Martian finds back into circulation, or sell high-demand resources where they are worth more. Fewer middlemen, more control over your own supply chain.
- **Off-grid contacts**  
Some Martian colonists operate outside standard communication channels. A drone can reach them, deploy a relay beacon, and open a direct line.  
That contact can unlock separate contracts, new dialogue paths, and trade routes that are not available through regular station operations.
- **Future upgrade potential**  
Drones can be upgraded through modular components. Shape it for stealth, cargo runs, scouting, signal relay, or survival in hostile zones.  
Different missions will reward different setups, so over time the drone becomes its own progression path, not just a side tool.
