
**WEAPON EXPERIMENTS (railgun, miner outpost)**

A live weapon test has been performed on a freshly developed “LRMD” (long range matter disintegrator) by CORE Arms. The spokesman Thomas Albertson had the following to say: “After a series of unfortunate setbacks we are finally ready to present a new weapon system for the Council navy. Its implication in future ship designs intimidates any pirate or criminal that would think about raiding. CORE Arms is still adamant on offering only the best solutions to those who fight for humanity”.  

Although such a statement could be considered vague at best, rumor has it that each of the major factions are already lining up to acquire the new weapon, before the “intended” buyer.

Our investigators were able to uncover information that these so-called “LRMDs” are a result of the continuation of **“Project Artemis”** that notoriously failed about **4 decades ago**, leading to catastrophic destruction on several test sites. Most information about the events of those years are still kept secret from the general public, but with the re-surfacing of that project we will try to keep you updated. Stay tuned!
