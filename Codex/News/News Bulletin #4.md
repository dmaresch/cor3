
**“The Voices”,  Cult of Mars, Radio transmissions**

Several ships passing near the orbit of Neptune have reported strange transmissions that have been recorded by the ships high frequency radio antennas. Although the recordings seem to be mostly corrupt and/or encrypted, it is clear that there is some sort of a voice trying to reach out. The origins of those transmissions remain unknown.

One of the ship captains, **Kumar Abdul** had something to say about it to our news crew: “We were passing near Neptune, as always, when suddenly our recording equipment woke up and started recording a signal. It is very strange, we do not know how it came to be. Very strange. Big mystery!” 

Although the situation has not been commented on by any authority, our tech expert Sean Bong reminds us that such equipment is considered outdated and is nothing more than slightly advanced radio receivers that were used for distress contacts. He also noted that this signal was likely **refracted** from Neptune’s atmosphere, while its origins are a mystery indeed.

Sean also stated that keeping in mind the cyclicity of the reports about those signals it’s likely that they are originating from one of the bigger “objects” that come into perfect position to be in range of the Neptune trade lane.
